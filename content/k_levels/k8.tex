\section{\texorpdfstring{$K_8$}{K_8} Overview}
\label{sec:k8-overview}

$K_8$ is the level of \emph{civilizational continua}: large-scale,
long-lived structures that arise when multiple $K_7$ social continua
stabilise shared institutions, infrastructures, codified norms,
collective memory mechanisms and large-scale coordination structures.

Distinctive features:
\begin{itemize}
    \item institutions as persistent operators,
    \item codified law and formal norms,
    \item economic and logistical infrastructures,
    \item large-scale information networks,
    \item symbolic systems (religion, science, ideology),
    \item historical cycles and path-dependence,
    \item multi-layered governance and authority.
\end{itemize}

$K_8$ is not reducible to the sum of $K_7$ groups: it has its own
admissible states, thresholds, cycles and collapse modes.

\begin{itemize}
