\subsubsection{Cycles on $K_{11}$}
\label{sec:cycles-k11}

The continuum $K_{11}$ corresponds to the level of \emph{meta-evolution}:
the domain where the evolution of higher-order operators, meta-logical
frameworks, meta-ontologies and meta-spaces becomes itself a structured and
dynamical process. If $K_{10}$ describes meta-models and universal operators,
then $K_{11}$ describes the \emph{evolution of the mechanisms} that govern
their transformation.

The state space:
\[
  \Omega(K_{11})
  =
  \{
    \text{meta-evolution rules},\;
    \text{evolution of universal operators},\;
    \text{meta-transformations of meta-logics},\;
    \text{evolution of meta-spaces},\;
    \text{adaptive higher-order constraints}
  \}.
\]

Axes:
\[
  A^{11}
  = \{
      \text{evolutionary axes over operators},\;
      \text{axes of meta-evolution of logics},\;
      \text{axes of meta-space adaptation},\;
      \text{axes of constraint evolution}
    \}.
\]

Potentials:
\[
  P^{11}
  = \{
      P^{11}_{\mathrm{meta\!-\!evol}},\;
      P^{11}_{\mathrm{adapt}},\;
      P^{11}_{\mathrm{universality}},\;
      P^{11}_{\mathrm{stability}}
    \}.
\]

Flows:
\[
  J^{11}
  = \{
      J^{11}_{\mathrm{evol}},\;
      J^{11}_{\mathrm{operator}},\;
      J^{11}_{\mathrm{meta\!-\!space}},\;
      J^{11}_{\mathrm{constraint}},\;
      J^{11}_{\mathrm{recursive}}
    \}.
\]

Thresholds:
\[
  \Theta_{11}
   =
   \{
     \Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{evol}},\;
     \Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{meta}},\;
     \Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{univ}},\;
     \Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{dim}}
   \}.
\]

The continuum exists if:
\[
  T_{11} < \min(\Theta_{11}), \qquad k(K_{11})>0.
\]


\subsubsection{Overview}

Cycles on $K_{11}$ represent higher-order dynamical systems that govern
how universal operators evolve, how meta-logics adapt to new meta-levels,
how meta-spaces grow, and how constraints reorganise under the pressure of
structural tension $T_{11}$. They form the backbone of the ``meta-evolutionary
architecture'' of the Ontology of Continua.

The defining characteristic of $K_{11}$ cycles is that they describe
\emph{evolution of the mechanisms of evolution} themselves.

This introduces a recursive depth not present in $K_{10}$, where operators
and meta-logics evolve but their evolutionary rules are fixed.  
In $K_{11}$ the rules themselves become variable and dynamic.


\subsubsection{1. The Meta-Evolutionary Operator Cycle $C_{\mathrm{evol-op}}$}

This cycle governs the evolution of universal operators ($U$, $\Psi$, $\Phi$,
$\Lambda$, $\Chi$) at the level of their transformation rules.

\[
  \mathrm{Rule\;definition}
  \to
  \mathrm{Operator\;evolution}
  \to
  \mathrm{Meta\!-\!evaluation}
  \to
  \mathrm{Rule\;revision}
  \to
  \mathrm{Rule\;definition}.
\]

Form:
\[
  C_{\mathrm{evol-op}}
  =
  \{
    A^{11}_{\mathrm{operator}},\;
    J^{11}_{\mathrm{operator}},\;
    P^{11}_{\mathrm{universality}},\;
    \Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{univ}}
  \}.
\]

Stability:
\[
  P^{11}_{\mathrm{universality}}>\Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{univ}}.
\]

\subsubsection{Cycle Metrics on $K_{11}$}

\paragraph{Length.}
\[
  L(C)=\oint d\Omega(K_{11}).
\]

\paragraph{Efficiency.}
\[
  C_{\mathrm{eff}}
  =\frac{\oint J^{11}\cdot dA^{11}}{L(C)}.
\]

\paragraph{Stability.}
\[
  S(C)
  =\min_{t\in C} d(\Omega(K_{11}),\partial\Omega(K_{11})).
\]

\paragraph{Weight.}
\[
  w(C)
  =F\!\big(
        C_{\mathrm{eff}},\;
        S(C),\;
        P^{11}_{\mathrm{meta\!-\!evol}},\;
        P^{11}_{\mathrm{universality}}
      \big).
\]


\subsubsection{Collapse of $K_{11}$ Cycles}

Collapse occurs when meta-evolutionary stability fails:

\[
   P^{11}_{\mathrm{adapt}} < \Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{evol}},\qquad
   P^{11}_{\mathrm{universality}} < \Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{univ}},\qquad
   P^{11}_{\mathrm{stability}} < \Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{meta}}.
\]

Then:
\[
  C_j\to\varnothing,\qquad
  \Omega(K_{11})=\varnothing,\qquad
  k(K_{11})\to 0.
\]


\subsubsection{Continuity from $K_{10}$ to $K_{11}$}

The transition requires:
\[
  T_{10}>\Theta^{11}_{\mathrm{dim}},
\]
which forces new axes associated with meta-evolution of operators and
meta-spaces:

\[
  A^{11}_{\mathrm{meta\!-\!logic}},\;
  A^{11}_{\mathrm{meta\!-\!space}},\;
  A^{11}_{\mathrm{constraint}},\;
  A^{11}_{\mathrm{operator}}.
\]

The result is a continuum of evolving meta-rules.
