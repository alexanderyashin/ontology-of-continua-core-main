\section{$M_9$ Overview}
\label{sec:m9-overview}

$M_9$ is the meta-space that enables the existence of 
theoretical continua ($K_9$). 
While $M_8$ provides the material, symbolic and institutional 
substrate of civilizations, $M_9$ introduces the structural conditions 
for the emergence, coexistence and transformation of 
scientific, mathematical and conceptual models.

In $M_9$, the fundamental units are not agents, infrastructures or
institutions, but \emph{models}, represented as structured objects 
with internal logics, constraints and transformation rules.  

Thus $M_9$ is the minimal space where \emph{theory} becomes an admissible continuum.

% ---------------------------------------------------------------
\subsection*{1. Admissible Configuration Space $\Omega(M_9)$}

\[
\Omega(M_9)=
\left\{
(\mathcal{M},\ \mathrm{Mor},\ A_{\mathrm{model}},\ 
\Theta_{\mathrm{coh}},\ C_{\mathrm{theory}},\ \Sigma_9)
\mid \text{coherence conditions hold}
\right\}.
\]

Components:

\begin{itemize}
    \item $\mathcal{M}$ — the set of admissible models 
        (mathematical, physical, chemical, biological, cognitive, social),
    \item $\mathrm{Mor}$ — morphisms between models 
        (interpretations, reductions, equivalences, functors),
    \item $A_{\mathrm{model}}$ — axes of theoretical variation,
    \item $\Theta_{\mathrm{coh}}$ — coherence thresholds,
    \item $C_{\mathrm{theory}}$ — cycles of theoretical refinement,
    \item $\Sigma_9$ — global structural constraints on theory-space.
\end{itemize}

Admissibility requires:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item existence of stable models: $\mathcal{M}\neq\emptyset$,
    \item existence of meaningful morphisms between models,
    \item non-trivial theoretical axes,
    \item coherence: $d_{\mathrm{coh}}(M_i, M_j)<\Theta_{\mathrm{coh}}$ for admissible pairs,
    \item possibility of theoretical evolution under operator $E$.
\end{enumerate}

\[
A(M_9)=

A_{\mathrm{syntax}},\
A_{\mathrm{semantics}},\
A_{\mathrm{abstraction}},\
A_{\mathrm{idealization}},\
A_{\mathrm{approximation}},\
A_{\mathrm{domain}},\
A_{\mathrm{logic}}

\]

Descriptions:

\begin{itemize}
    \item $A_{\mathrm{syntax}}$ — formal languages, symbolic structures,
    \item $A_{\mathrm{semantics}}$ — interpretation spaces,
    \item $A_{\mathrm{abstraction}}$ — degrees of generality,
    \item $A_{\mathrm{idealization}}$ — allowed simplifications,
    \item $A_{\mathrm{approximation}}$ — error and convergence structures,
    \item $A_{\mathrm{domain}}$ — domain-specific axes (physics, chemistry, biology…),
    \item $A_{\mathrm{logic}}$ — logical rules, inference systems.
\end{itemize}

A necessary condition for model-theoretic life:

\[
\dim(A_{\mathrm{syntax}})\ge 1,
\quad
\dim(A_{\mathrm{semantics}})\ge 1.
\]

    \item $F_{\mathrm{coherence}}$ — potential keeping models mutually interpretable,

\[
F_{\mathrm{coherence}}>\Theta_{\mathrm{fragmentation}},
\qquad
F_{\mathrm{consistency}}>\Theta_{\mathrm{paradox}}.
\]

% ---------------------------------------------------------------
\subsection*{4. Thresholds $\Theta(M_9)$}

Key coherence thresholds:

\begin{itemize}
    \item $\Theta_{\mathrm{coh}}$ — maximal allowable divergence for models to coexist,
    \item $\Theta_{\mathrm{consistency}}$ — threshold of internal logical stability,
    \item $\Theta_{\mathrm{predictive}}$ — minimal predictive accuracy,
    \item $\Theta_{\mathrm{paradox}}$ — boundary where inconsistency collapses the continuum,
    \item $\Theta_{\mathrm{fragmentation}}$ — threshold for theoretical disintegration.
\end{itemize}

Crossing $\Theta_{\mathrm{paradox}}$ forces collapse of $\Omega(K_9)$,
analogous to institutional collapse in $K_8$.

\subsection*{6. Cycles $C(M_9)$}

Fundamental cycles:

\begin{itemize}
    \item $C_{\mathrm{theory}}$ 
        — hypothesis $\to$ derivation $\to$ evaluation $\to$ revision,
    \item $C_{\mathrm{abstraction}}$ 
        — concrete $\to$ abstract $\to$ generalized $\to$ instantiated,
    \item $C_{\mathrm{interpretation}}$ 
        — model $\to$ mapping $\to$ reinterpretation,
    \item $C_{\mathrm{coherence}}$ 
        — local fits $\to$ global consistency $\to$ refit cycles,
    \item $C_{\mathrm{unification}}$ 
        — domain models $\to$ cross-domain structure $\to$ unified framework.
\end{itemize}

A living theoretical continuum requires:

\[
\oint_{C_{\mathrm{coherence}}} dA \approx 0.
\]

% ---------------------------------------------------------------
\subsection*{7. Boundary $\partial\Omega(M_9)$}

Conditions approaching the boundary:

\begin{itemize}
    \item breakdown of inference closure,
    \item loss of coherence: $d_{\mathrm{coh}}>\Theta_{\mathrm{coh}}$,
    \item proliferation of paradoxes,
    \item divergence of formal languages beyond mutual interpretability,
    \item failure of model–world correspondence (predictive collapse),
    \item runaway theoretical tension $T_9$.
\end{itemize}

Crossing $\partial\Omega(M_9)$ collapses theory to fragmented $M_8$-admissible structures.

\[
A_{\mathrm{syntax}}\neq\emptyset,
\quad
A_{\mathrm{semantics}}\neq\emptyset,
\quad
\Theta_{\mathrm{coh}}\ \text{finite}.
\]

Symbolic and scientific structures of $M_8$ lift into  
model spaces of $M_9$ via the operator $\Psi$.

$M_{10}$ introduces:

\begin{itemize}
    \item categories of model-categories,
    \item functorial dynamics,
    \item meta-coherence thresholds,
    \item cycles of self-description,
    \item the space where meta-theories themselves form continua.
\end{itemize}

Thus $M_9$ provides the structured model-space that becomes the substrate for $K_{10}$.
